Project Healm

Information

Our website - "Healm" will allow the user to record their day to day by keeping track of actions and how these actions affect their physical and mental well being over time. Users will be able to create new actions that repeat over some time interval, and over each time interval the user will enter relevant data related to that action. ome data will likely be fixed for any created actions, such as ranking how one feels mentally or physically on a scale of 1-10, and possibly an area for notes that can expand on nuances not able to be expressed through a simple numerical scale. The ability to create custom data fields may be desirable as well. The goal here is to give users a better understanding of how various actions affect their lives on a day to day, as well as long term, basis in a more concrete and analytical way. Functionality to create groups may be added later, wherein certain actions will be ascribed by the group owner to its members, and the effects of these actions can be analyzed by said owner. This may possibly be of utility to those conducting research, or even in a less professional setting like a personal trainer keeping track of how effective their exercise regimen is for their clients.

The Main Features of Healm

  • Upload Data From Smart Watch - User connects their smartwatch to sync health information for the Healm account
  • Daily Report - User gets daily reports of health information, such as the number of steps taken
  • View Charts - The user views charts of past input data to see progress over time
  • Send Message - The user sends a message to another Healm user
  • View Messages - The user views messages from other Healm users
  • Create Group - User creates a group of Healm users for collaboration on health goals
  • Join Group - The user joins a group
  • View Group - User views the stats of a group that they are a member of
  • Leave Group - User leaves a group that they are a member of
  • Set Group Goals - Member of a group sets a goal for all of the members of the group
